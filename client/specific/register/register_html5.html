Real-time client-side form validation: HTML5

HTML5 offers some of the tools needed for this purpose. Unfortunately, alone it's not enough. We're missing a way of deciding whether or not an input-element has been touched (like the AngularJS "ng-pristine" class). On top of this we're also missing a way of comparing the user input of different input-elements (e.g. e-mail and confirm e-mail) and there's no way to disable the submit button until all required input elements have been filled in/checked. It is also worth noting that the solution isn't fully compatible with the Chrome Autofill functionality (the Bootstrap "has-success" glyphicons disappears when it is used).
